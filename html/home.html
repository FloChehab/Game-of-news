Game of News

Explore the news in a different way

Game of news is a data visualization platform that aims at exploring the latent ties between news outlets.

Itâ€™s based upon the GDELT (Global Database of Events, Language, and Tone) project and in particular the events and mentions information it gathers. In fact, GDELT is constantly monitoring thousands and thousands of news related website to keep track of what events are mentioned in what articles. It also provides the estimated tone of the article.

We have aggregated all that information and summarized it in two different views:

  • A two-level graph that relates the news outlets based on the news events they share in common.
  • A two-level stacked area chart that shows how these relationships evolve over time.
